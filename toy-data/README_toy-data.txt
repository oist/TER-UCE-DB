## random-20-loci-aln-75p.tar.gz
20 randomly selected loci from 75%-occupancy alignments produced by "phyluce_align_get_only_loci_with_min_taxa" with phyluce v1.6.6
Data from: Contribution #1 to UCE database
Can be directly accessed through command:
wget -O - https://raw.githubusercontent.com/sihellem/TER-UCE-DB/main/toy-data/random-20-loci-aln-75p.tar.gz | tar xzvf -
